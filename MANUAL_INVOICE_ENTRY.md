# ✅ MANUAL INVOICE ENTRY ADDED!

## 🎯 Complete Invoice Creation Flow

### New 4-Step Process:

#### Step 1: Choose Invoice Type
- **Client Invoice (Income)** - Green, for invoices you send
- **Supplier Invoice (Expense)** - Amber, for bills you receive

#### Step 2: Choose Entry Method (NEW!)
- **Upload Document** 
  - Upload PDF or image
  - AI extracts all data automatically
  - Badge: "AI Powered"
  
- **Create Manually** 
  - Enter all details yourself
  - No document needed
  - Badge: "Manual Entry"

#### Step 3: Upload or Skip
- **If Upload**: Upload document, AI analyzes
- **If Manual**: Skip directly to Step 4

#### Step 4: Review & Save
- Shows invoice type badge
- Shows entry method badge (AI Extracted vs Manual Entry)
- Fill in or verify all fields
- Save to database

### Complete User Flows:

**Flow 1: AI-Powered Upload**
